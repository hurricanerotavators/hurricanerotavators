About us

Focus Manufacturing Company is a leading exporter and manufacturer of Agriculture parts and Machinery since 2014 founded in Ludhiana, Punjab by JPM group. Initially, the company focused on export of agriculture parts to various other countries across the globe and now entering the indian market with its superior products.

Focus Manufacturing Copany is the parent company of Hurricane Rotavators. The company is aggresively creating its network of dealers all around India with the prime goal to satisfy the need of farmers at affordable costs and help farm tech to flourish.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing unit is equipped with advanced machinery capable of executing bulk orders with precision. We use best quality of raw material and sustainable engineering designs for manufacturing innovative implements of International standards.

Our Vision

We respect Farmers for the work they do and for the commitment they have towards their work. Our vision is to make a difference and give farmers the tools they need at the most affordable prices. We are continuously working to see the Farm Tech sector flourish. As Business value can not only be achieved with technology our commitment is to ensure best service possible to farmers. With our superior quality products we hope to create an impact on the life of farmers.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to give farmers the tools they need to continue feeding us at the most affordable prices. We want to grow together with farmers and become the No. 1 Manufacturers of Agriculture Impliments in India.
